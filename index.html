Cheerleading

team activity in which elements of dance and acrobatics are combined with shouted slogans in order to entertain spectators at sporting events and to encourage louder and more enthusiastic cheering

Nominations

  • co-ed - team consisting both male and female athletes
  • all-girl teams - team consisting of only female athletes
  • cheer stunts - a group of 4 people, performing complicated stunts with 1 flyer
  • partner stunts - 2 people, 1 base and 1 flyer, performing complicated stunts
